About Us: The Wiki, The Creator, and The Concept

What is the Wiki?

The Alternative Medicine Wiki is a wiki website made for young learners interested in alternative and complementary medicine! The wiki contains basic information about some forms and types of alternative medicine (e.g. Aromatherapy, Herbal Medicine, etc.). A special thing about this wiki is that each lesson has a few simple notes and interpretations made by the website creator Marthea (Pengu-nim) herself! Just click [Pengu-nim’s Note], and you’ll find a “for dummies” interpretation. (CLICK NOTE!!)

What Was It Made For?

Originally, the website was only made as Marthea (Pengu-nim)’s Computer Science 3 Quarter Project. She was assigned the topic: “Alternative Medicine: Exploring complementary and alternative approaches to healthcare.” Eventually, she had fun coding and designing the website, so she continued.

Soon enough, The Alternative Medicine Wiki was born! Now, it is made to inform, share, and engage users about healthy alternative approaches to modern healthcare.

(Hey, Mother Nature is still here to heal you, y’know?)

The Concept

At first, when Marthea (Pengu-nim) was given the topic, she thought: “alternative medicine : herbs and leaves”. So, she designed the website with “herbs, leaves, and trees” in mind. This is why the whole website looks like it has “trees” as a concept. Soon enough, Marthea (Pengu-nim) realized,

“Oh, wow, there’s also other types lol”.

Now, if you click on Lessons, it’s a rainbow(?) of colors still following the Alternative Medicine Concept.
